The Description

Each member, in our computer science class, took data on their happiness throughout the school day. The data that is shown in the graph was collected over the period of two weeks. Each dot on the graph represents the amount of people recorded in the average of happiness(color of the dot on a scale from yellow to red). On the Y-scale on can find the different subjects, which are all represented as numbers on the scale(these numbers correlate to the numbers right about the graph), on the X-scale on can find the days of the week and period of the day.
